Thinksday

weekly reflections by Nat Tupper

"For the past couple of years, I've been taking photos of a different bird each week and texting it to my friend, Binks. Well, things have changed a bit recently and I decided to turn Birdsday into 'Thinksday' — a photo, a thought, a brief reflection or story. Why should I let fawning fidelity to facts ruin an otherwise good story?"  — Nat
